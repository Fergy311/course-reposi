Shearer's

Named after football Legend Alan Shearer and located on the ground of ST jameses park itself offering up a wide selection on beers, spirits, wine and cocktails along with a varied menue including a renowned sunday lunch. With the choice of an outside terrace, for those wishing to dine al fresco, or inside where you can watch all of the latest sporting fixtures on our large screens throughout the bar, Shearer's Bar is the perfect place to enjoy a leisurely lunch or party the night away. Families are welcome until 10pm Monday to Saturday and until 8pm on Sunday. A children's menu is available until this time.

Trillians

A cellar bar hidden away in Princess Square, just opposite the city’s main library, Trillians is famed across the land for it’s great atmosphere and varied live shows throughout the year. With regular Rock, Metal, Punk, Hardcore, Reggae, Glam and many other shows you can guarantee an eclectic mix

Dog & Parrot

known around Newcastle as a great student bar for the brilliant weekend atmosphere and having some of the lowest prices for drinks in the town, during the day they offer a food menue The Dog & Parrot is an independent pub with a terrace bar in Newcastle city centre which is perfect for grabbing a pint with your mates or trying one of their creative cocktails. Tuck into a juicy plant based burger from the city's newest flippers - Naughty By Nature from 12pm - 8pm every day.

Alchemist

A cocktail bar and restaurant unlike any other! Newcastle is legendary as one the world’s most vibrant and exciting cities. In fact, As the original frontier between roman civilisation & the mystical wilderness beyond, it’s been the place where magic happens for centuries. Now we’re casting our own spell on this great destination at our Eldon Square home, right at the heart of the city. Here’s where you’ll find the most devilishly dark drinks and a dazzlingly different dining experience – all served up with a distinctly theatrical flavour, by staff dedicated to giving you the most entertaining service you’ll ever enjoy.

Greys club

Located on the historic Grey's street in the heart of the city, Greys club offers up a different experience just about every night you can visit, with swingers a groovy disco taking the tuesday and thursday slots, along with the new comer KODA on a friday night playing club remixes and house tunes, followed by the legendary RISE every saturday playing a different vibe in each room with the main room blasting all maner of metal and emo tunes.

Tup Tup Palace

Newcastle's No.1 Spot to go, by far one of the most popular nightclubs to go in newcastle offering up weekly events such as "persistence" which offers up RnB, hip-hop and afrobeats tunes. Along with clubnights Tup Tup also offers cocktail classes and a private hire bar on the roof top that comes equipped with booths and a smoking area

Digital

Another popular spot in town, Digital offers up a multitude of DJ's everyweek playing everything from disco remixes to showcases of the artists talents, with fairly priced drinks and an amazing sound system this place is popular with college and uni students and adults looking for a great night out.

Rusty's

Rusty's is a staple of Newcastles LGBTQA+ scene, with user-reviewed "top noch staff and average priced drinks", also on offer at Rusty's are amazing Guest drag DJ's offering up a different vibe everynight regular acts such as Gloria Hole, playing what they call "Cheese and Chart" music and plastiQ playing all the hits.
