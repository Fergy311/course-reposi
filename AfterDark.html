Bars about Toon

Shearer's

Named after football Legend Alan Shearer and located on the ground of ST jameses park itself offering up a wide selection on beers, spirits, wine and cocktails along with a varied menue including a renowned sunday lunch. With the choice of an outside terrace, for those wishing to dine al fresco, or inside where you can watch all of the latest sporting fixtures on our large screens throughout the bar, Shearer's Bar is the perfect place to enjoy a leisurely lunch or party the night away. Families are welcome until 10pm Monday to Saturday and until 8pm on Sunday. A children's menu is available until this time.

Trillians >

A cellar bar hidden away in Princess Square, just opposite the city’s main library, Trillians is famed across the land for it’s great atmosphere and varied live shows throughout the year. With regular Rock, Metal, Punk, Hardcore, Reggae, Glam and many other shows you can guarantee an eclectic mix

Dog & Parrot

The Dog & Parrot is an independent Rock ‘n’ Roll bar & terrace in the heart of Newcastle, just a stones throw from Central Station.

Alchemist
